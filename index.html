About

Boycott Woke is a database tracking major companies and individuals involved in "woke" initiatives. Alongside this resource, we coordinate targeted boycott campaigns against selected companies during specific time periods.

Our focus is on companies engaging in the most extreme initiatives. When a company is selected, we will formally request they dismantle the relevant programs. If they refuse, we will proceed with the boycott.

We recognize that avoiding certain stores indefinitely may not be practical. To address this, our campaigns are time-limited, enabling participants to plan alternatives while ensuring their efforts have maximum impact.

No sign-up is required. Boycott Woke is a platform that organizes information and delivers direct notifications about upcoming actions.

Why?

The West has become unrecognizable. What used to be our home has devolved into an economic zone for Asia and the Middle East.

All cultural guardrails have been removed. Leaving our youth to wander into the arms of nihilism, pessimism and depression.

This imported division has torn apart our societies. Investment in our young and poor has been halted for the enrichment of the established and "oppressed".

False claims by social constructionists have dismantled the meaning of being a man and a woman. Encouraging arrangements perverse to our human nature.

With freedoms being stripped away every day, it seems time is limited for taking action against this decline.

Boycott Woke hopes to address the political inaction on tackling our most pressing cultural issues.

By coordinating efforts we believe we can put up an effective fight to regain our homes and future.
